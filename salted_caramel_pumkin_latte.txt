INGREDIENTS
2 cups fat free milk
¼ cup canned pumpkin puree
6 Tablespoons caramel ice cream topping, divided
2 Tablespoons vanilla extract
½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 cup strong brewed coffee
½ cup Cool Whip
coarse ground sea salt



INSTRUCTIONS
1. In a small saucepan, combine the milk, pumpkin, 4 Tablespoons caramel, vanilla, and pumpkin pie spice. Stir until the mixture comes to a very slight boil.
2. Remove from the heat immediately and pour the mixture into a blender. Pulse for about 2 minutes or until very frothy.
3. Divide the milk into 2 large mugs. Pour ½ cup coffee into each mug.
4. Top with Cool Whip, extra caramel, and sea salt. Makes 2 servings.
