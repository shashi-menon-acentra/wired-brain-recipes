INGREDIENTS
8-12 ounces freshly brewed coffee
1 0.5 teaspoons coconut oil
½ cup warm coconut milk from carton
⅛ teaspoon cinnamon optional
⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper optional

INSTRUCTIONS
1. Pour hot coffee into a large mug along with the coconut oil. Use a frother to blend the coffee with the coconut oil until the mixture lightens in color and becomes frothy, about 1-2 minutes.
2. Add any extra ingredients you’d like including warm coconut milk, cinnamon and/or cayenne pepper and give it a quick blend for an additional 30 seconds.
3. Serve immediately.
